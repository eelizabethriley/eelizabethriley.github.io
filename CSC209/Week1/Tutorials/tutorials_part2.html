HTML Styles and Text Formatting

Here are some of the styles you can apply to elements in HTML. There are various ways to format text. You can make text bold or indicate that it is important with the strong tag, which also renders as bold text by default. You can italicize the name of a book or emphasize text with a certain mood. You can also make text small or mark it. There's also a strikethrough and an underline. A subscript and superscript is available as well.
The text direction can be changes as well.
