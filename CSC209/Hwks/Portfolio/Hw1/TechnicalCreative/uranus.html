About Uranus

Description

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and the third largest in the Solar System. It has an orbital period of 370 days. There are 28 known moons orbiting Uranus, and 5 are named after characters in literature.

Moons

Here is a list of the major 5 moons orbiting Uranus.

  • Ariel
  • Umbriel
