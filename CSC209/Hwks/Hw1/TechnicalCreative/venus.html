About Venus

Description

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It has an orbital period of 583 days. The background image of this paragraph displays the surface of the Eistla region of Venus which has "pancake domes" formed due to volcanic activity. There are also many impact craters on the surface of Venus which are well preserved due to its historical volcanism. Venus is my favorite planet personally (other than Earth) :D
